The Atelier

Born from a love
of clothes that fit.

Stitch Awesome was built on a simple, stubborn idea — that everyone deserves clothes that actually fit, at prices that don't insult them.

For more than twenty years, our team of master seamstresses has reshaped wedding gowns, rescued vintage coats, taken in dream dresses, and saved countless suits the night before the big day. The work is quiet. The results aren't.

Walk in. Try it on. We'll show you exactly how it could fit — and what it would cost — before you ever commit. No appointments. No pressure. Just craft.

Wedding Gowns

Formal & Bridesmaids

Dresses & Skirts
